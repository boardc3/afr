01 — Mission

Our Mission

America’s First Responders was created to support the men and women who dedicate themselves to serving others.

Our mission is to strengthen volunteer fire departments, EMS agencies, rescue squads, law enforcement organizations, and other first responder communities.

We achieve this through meaningful partnerships, valuable resources, innovative programs, and community support. By easing the burdens on local departments, we help ensure that those who protect our communities are themselves protected, supported, and valued.

03 — Leadership

About the Founder

Michael Faust

Founder, America's First Responders
40 Years of Service 4th-Gen Firefighter MSFA Hall of Fame Kent & Queen Anne's Hall of Fame

Michael Faust is the founder of America’s First Responders.

A fourth-generation volunteer firefighter with nearly 40 years of service, Michael created America’s First Responders to help strengthen volunteer organizations and support the men and women who dedicate themselves to serving their communities.

Michael has held leadership positions at the local, county, and state levels, including serving as President of the Maryland State Firefighters Association during the COVID-19 pandemic. He has been inducted into both the Maryland State Firefighters Association Hall of Fame and the Kent & Queen Anne’s Volunteer Fire and EMS Hall of Fame.

Professionally, Michael has spent more than three decades helping businesses grow through marketing, technology, communications, and strategic partnerships. His passion for service, entrepreneurship, and community involvement continues to drive the mission behind America’s First Responders.

Michael and his wife, Stacey, are proud parents, grandparents, and lifelong supporters of the communities they call home.
